Invitation Writing Guide

Instructions for Writing Effective Invitations:

  1. Clear heading -- State the purpose of the invitation (e.g., "Wedding Invitation").
  2. Host information -- Include who is extending the invitation.
  3. Honorifics -- Use proper titles (Mr., Mrs., Dr.) where appropriate.
  4. Event details -- Clearly state date, time, and venue.
  5. Special instructions -- Include dress code, RSVP details, or other requirements.
  6. Polite tone -- Use courteous language throughout.
  7. RSVP information -- Provide clear response instructions.
  8. Keep it concise -- Aim for about 100 words.
  9. Visual hierarchy -- Make important details stand out.
  10. Proofread carefully -- Ensure perfect grammar and spelling.
