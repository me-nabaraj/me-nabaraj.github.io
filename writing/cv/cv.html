CV Writing Guide

Instructions for Writing an Effective CV:

  1. Header -- Include your name, contact information, and professional title.
  2. Professional Summary -- Write a brief 3-4 sentence overview of your qualifications.
  3. Work Experience -- List in reverse chronological order with achievements.
  4. Education -- Include degrees, institutions, and graduation years.
  5. Skills -- Highlight relevant hard and soft skills.
  6. Certifications -- Include any professional certifications.
  7. Projects -- Mention significant projects (if relevant).
  8. Use Action Verbs -- Begin bullet points with strong action words.
  9. Keep It Concise -- Aim for 1-2 pages maximum.
  10. Tailor to Position -- Customize for each job application.
  11. Proofread Carefully -- Ensure perfect grammar and formatting.
