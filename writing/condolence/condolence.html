Condolence Writing Guide

Instructions for Writing a Good Condolence:

  1. Header -- First, write the header like Hearty Condolence or heartfelt Condolence.
  2. Photo placeholder -- Include a placeholder for the actual photo of the deceased.
  3. Border -- All your content should be enclosed with borders.
  4. Express Grief -- Begin with a heartfelt expression of sorrow for the loss.
  5. Mention the Cause (If Appropriate) -- If relevant, mention it briefly but sensitively.
  6. Praise the Person -- Highlight the positive qualities of the deceased.
  7. Offer Condolences to the Bereaved -- Express sympathy and support to the grieving family.
  8. Wish for the Departed Soul -- Conclude with a respectful prayer or wish for peace.
  9. Use a Respectful Tone -- Keep the language formal yet sincere.
  10. Keep It Brief and Meaningful -- Typically around 80-120 words.
  11. Check for Grammar and Clarity -- Ensure the message is grammatically correct.

Hearty Condolence

Photo

Late Rajnarayan Tripathi

I am deeply saddened by the tragic passing away of Mr. Rajnarayan Tripathi in a car accident at the age of 52. It is heartbreaking to lose such a dedicated and compassionate individual so suddenly.

He was not only a wonderful teacher but also a committed social worker who always extended his help to those in need. His kindness, cooperation, and selfless service left a lasting impact on many lives.

My heartfelt condolences go out to his family and loved ones. May they find strength and comfort in this difficult time.

May his noble soul rest in eternal peace.

Ravi Joshi

Vice Principal

Green Valley English School

Heartfelt Condolence

Photo

Late Rajnarayan Tripathi

I am profoundly saddened by the untimely demise of Rajnarayan Tripathi, who tragically lost his life in a car accident at the age of 52. His sudden departure is an immense loss to all who knew him.

Mr. Tripathi was an exemplary educator and a dedicated social worker, known for his kindness, cooperation, and unwavering commitment to the betterment of society. His contributions will be remembered
with deep respect and admiration.

I extend my heartfelt condolences to his bereaved family and loved ones. May they find strength and solace in this difficult time.

May his departed soul rest in eternal peace.

Srijan Tamang

Managing Director

Innovative Development Bank
