Advertisement Writing (For Exams)

Advertisement writing is a form of persuasive writing asked in secondary level examinations. It aims to attract attention and deliver information in a short and effective way.

Purpose

To inform and attract customers.

Length

Usually 60–80 words.

Language

Simple, positive, and catchy.

Format

Headline, details, contact.

In exams, marks are given for format, clarity, brevity, and attractive presentation.

Standard Components of an Advertisement

  • Headline: Grabs attention instantly
  • Tagline / Slogan: Creates recall value
  • Description: Highlights features and benefits
  • Offer: Discounts, guarantees, or advantages
  • Call to Action: Encourages immediate response
  • Contact Details: Address, phone, website
