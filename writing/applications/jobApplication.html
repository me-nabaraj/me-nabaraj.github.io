Professional Job Application Writing

Master the art of writing compelling job applications for SEE Examination
Key to Professional Success

Essential Elements

  • Sender's address & contact details
  • Date of application
  • Recipient's address
  • Clear subject line
  • Professional salutation
  • Body with clear paragraphs
  • Polite closing
  • Signature and name

Key Guidelines

  • Be concise and professional
  • Tailor to specific job
  • Highlight relevant skills
  • Use formal language
  • Check spelling and grammar
  • Include references if possible
  • State availability clearly
  • Proofread multiple times

Powerful Phrases

  • "I am writing to apply for..."
  • "With reference to your advertisement..."
  • "I believe I am suitable because..."
  • "My qualifications include..."
  • "I have experience in..."
  • "I am available for an interview..."
  • "Looking forward to hearing from you..."
  • "Thank you for considering my application"
