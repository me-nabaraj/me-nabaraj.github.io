How to Write a Job Application

  • Begin with the correct address of the employer.
  • Write a clear subject mentioning the post.
  • Introduce yourself briefly in the first paragraph.
  • Mention your qualification, skills, and experience.
  • State why you are suitable for the job.
  • Close politely and express hope for consideration.
  • Keep the total length around 150 words.
