How to Write a Leave Application (SEE Focus)

  • Follow a formal format: address, subject, salutation, body, and closing.
  • Clearly mention the reason for leave and the duration.
  • Use polite and respectful language.
  • Keep it brief and relevant. Avoid unnecessary details.
  • Word limit should not exceed 150 words.
  • End with a proper closing like Yours obediently or Sincerely.
✍️ SEE Tip: Marks are awarded for format, clarity, grammar, and relevance.
